## ğŸš€ How to Use CampusCycle

### Getting Started

1. **Visit the Website**
   - Go to your CampusCycle website (deployed on Render)
   - Click "Sign Up" to create an account

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Use your **.edu email address** (required for verification)
   - Choose your campus from the list
   - Set a password (must be at least 8 characters)
   - Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

3. **Start Browsing**
   - Explore the marketplace to see what's available
   - Use filters to find specific items
   - Click on any listing to see full details and photos

### Buying Items

1. **Browse the Marketplace**
   - Use the search bar to find specific items
   - Filter by category, condition, price range, or giveaway status
   - Click on a listing you're interested in

2. **View Listing Details**
   - See photos, description, price, and condition
   - Check the seller's information
   - View location and availability

3. **Contact the Seller**
   - Click "Message Seller" to start a conversation
   - Ask questions about the item
   - Arrange pickup or delivery

4. **Complete the Purchase**
   - Work out payment details with the seller (cash, Venmo, etc.)
   - Mark the item as "Claimed" when you agree to buy it

### Selling Items

1. **Create a Listing**
   - Click the "+ New Listing" button
   - Fill in the item details:
     - Title (e.g., "MacBook Pro 13\" - Excellent Condition")
     - Description (be detailed and honest!)
     - Category (textbooks, electronics, furniture, etc.)
     - Condition (excellent, good, fair, poor)
     - Price (or mark as free giveaway)
     - Location and zipcode (optional)
   - Upload up to 5 photos (more photos = more interest!)
   - Click "Create Listing"

2. **Manage Your Listings**
   - Go to your Profile to see all your listings
   - Edit listings if needed
   - Mark items as "Sold" when they're gone
   - Delete listings for items you no longer have

3. **Respond to Messages**
   - Check your Inbox for messages from interested buyers
   - Answer questions promptly
   - Arrange meetup times and locations

### Giveaways

- Mark items as "Free" when creating a listing
- Help other students by giving away items you no longer need
- Free items are highlighted with a "FREE" badge in the marketplace
