## How It Works

### Buying Items

1. Browse the marketplace and use filters to narrow down your search
2. Click on a listing you're interested in to see details and photos
3. Click "Message Seller" to start a conversation
4. Chat with the seller about the item, price, and meeting details
5. Arrange a safe meeting spot on campus to exchange the item

### Selling Items

1. Click "New Listing" from the marketplace
2. Add photos of your item (up to 5 photos)
3. Fill in the details:
   - Title and description
   - Category (textbooks, furniture, etc.)
   - Condition (excellent, good, fair, poor)
   - Price (or mark as free giveaway!)
   - Location (optional)
4. Set status to "Available"
5. Respond to messages from interested buyers
6. Update status to "Claimed" when someone is getting it
7. Mark as "Sold" when the transaction is complete

### Giving Items Away

1. Create a listing as usual
2. Check the "This is a giveaway (free)" box
3. Set the price to $0 (or it will be set automatically)
4. Wait for students to message you
5. Arrange pickup or meeting on campus

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to verify my email?
Yes! We verify all accounts with .edu email addresses to keep the community safe and exclusive to students.

### Can I list items from other campuses?
No, you can only see and interact with listings from students at your own campus. This keeps things local and safe.

### Is there a fee to use CampusCycle?
No! CampusCycle is completely free to use. No listing fees, no transaction fees.

### How do I know if someone is trustworthy?
- All users are verified with .edu emails
- You can see other students' profiles
- Use the report feature if you encounter any issues
- Chat with sellers before meeting to get a feel for them

### What if I can't pick up an item right away?
You can mark items as "Claimed" when someone has committed to taking it. This lets other students know it's spoken for.

### Can I delete my account?
Contact support if you'd like to delete your account. You'll need to remove all your active listings first.
